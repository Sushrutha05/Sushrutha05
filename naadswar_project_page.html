NaadSwar

NaadSwar — Listen, learn, and align your pitch with the purity of Indian classical swars.

Download

Project Overview

NaadSwar is a real-time pitch detection app designed to help music learners tune their voice or instruments to the precise swaras of Indian classical music — Sa, Re, Ga, Ma, Pa, Dha, and Ni. The app combines traditional musical principles with modern technology, providing an intelligent and interactive way to practice riyaaz. Its clean, minimalistic interface ensures that learners can focus on their music while still accessing all the tools they need for effective practice.

One of NaadSwar’s standout features is its live histogram-style graph, which visualizes noise intensity and pitch stability in real-time. This feature allows users to immediately identify inconsistencies in their notes, monitor the strength of their voice or instrument, and make adjustments on the spot. By turning audio feedback into an intuitive visual representation, NaadSwar makes understanding pitch accuracy more engaging and easier to grasp.

Under the hood, the app uses live microphone input and frequency detection algorithms to map sounds to the closest swar, based on a user-defined base “Sa” frequency. This flexibility accommodates different vocal ranges and instruments, ensuring personalized and accurate guidance. NaadSwar also emphasizes progressive learning, giving users insights into their practice patterns, consistency, and tonal accuracy over time.

Looking ahead, NaadSwar aims to become a complete digital companion for Indian classical musicians. Planned enhancements include pitch tracking history, scale recognition, and adaptive tuning feedback, transforming it into a tool that not only detects pitch but actively guides learners to improve their tonal control, precision, and overall musicality. By blending technology, visual feedback, and musical tradition, NaadSwar empowers students, hobbyists, and professional musicians to elevate their practice and connect more deeply with the art of Indian classical music.

Key Features

  • Real-time pitch detection
  • Histogram graph showing noise intensity
  • Customizable base “Sa”
  • Immediate visual feedback on pitch accuracy
  • Planned pitch tracking and practice history
  • Planned scale recognition for advanced learning
  • Planned adaptive tuning guidance
